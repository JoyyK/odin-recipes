Description

What is mujadara? Good question. Learn yourselfOne of the easiest recipes to dip your toes into the flavor of brown rice. Hate its cardboard taste? Don't worry, the lentils and onions carry this recipe hard. Get a little freaky and branch out your palette by trying out this dish.

Ingredients

  • 4 garlic cloves, diced
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • Black pepper
  • 5 cups water
  • 1 cup brown rice
  • 1 cup lentils
  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • 2 yellow onions, halved and thinly sliced
  • 1 bundle green onions
  • 1/2 cup cilantro, chopped
  • Greek yogurt, for serving

Steps

  2. In a big boy pot add the garlic, bay leaves, cumin, salt, 20 twists of black pepper, and water. Bring it to a boil.
  3. Clean the rice and add it to the water once it's boiling. Cover, simmer, and cook for 10 minutes.
  4. Cut the onions. Don't cry, about a thing. Every thing will be alright.
  5. Heat up the olive oil (yes, all of it) in a pan. Make sure it's hot enough that dropping a piece of onion in it will start to sizzle and scream instantly. Once hot enough, dump the rest of the onions in.
  6. Around here it should have been around 10 minutes. Do this step once it has actually been 10 minutes. Add the lentils to the pot. Cover, simmer, and cook for another 20-23 minutes.
  7. Now it's time for the slow grind of frying the onions. Let them sit until they start to shrink. It should take about 20 minutes for them to caramelize, so be patient. If you think they're ready, think again. Let those suckers shrivel and brown until they no longer resemble an onion.
  8. Once the onions are done, sit them on a plate with paper towels. We want the oily juices to drain a bit. Lightly sprinkly salt on top of the onions. Let them crisp up and regret ever existing.
  9. During this time start chopping the green onions and cilantro.
  10. Once the rice + lentil combo is finished, add it into the pan the onions were cooking in. Mix the onions and greenies into the pan so everything is well-combined.
  11. Serve with some greek yogurt.
